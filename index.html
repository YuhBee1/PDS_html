Terms of Service
1. Services & Delivery

Paramount Digital Services agrees to deliver the services outlined in the signed proposal or invoice. Timelines begin once full payment or agreed deposit is received, and all required materials are provided by the client.

2. Payment Terms

All projects require a 50% deposit upfront. Final deliverables are released upon full payment. Retainer packages are billed monthly in advance. Late payments incur a 5% monthly fee after a 7-day grace period.

3. Revisions

Each package includes a specified number of revision rounds. Revisions beyond the included rounds are billed at $50/hour. Revisions must be requested within 7 days of delivery.

4. Intellectual Property

Upon final payment, the client receives full ownership of final deliverables. Paramount Digital Services retains the right to display the work in its portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

5. Cancellations & Refunds

Deposits are non-refundable once work has commenced. If a project is cancelled mid-way, the client owes payment for all work completed to date.

6. Confidentiality

We treat all client information as strictly confidential. We do not share, sell, or disclose client data or project details to third parties without written consent.
